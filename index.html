About

Chasen Jeffries is a PhD student studying International Political Economy and Computation Analytics at Claremont Graduate University. In 2019, he received a B.A in History from High Point University where he studied modern European and Asian history. His work uses a variety of quantitative and qualitative metholodogy to investigate a range of political economy questions including Intellectual Property Protections, Human Capital Accumulation, and International Political Economy. He has been published in the Journal of Policy Analysis and Management and the Columbia Journal of History. He has presented at conferences at Duke University, American Historical Association (AHA), and International Congress on Information and Communication Technology (ICICT).
