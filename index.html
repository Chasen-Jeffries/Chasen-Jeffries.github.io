About

Chasen Jeffries is a PhD student studying International Political Economy and Computation Analytics at Claremont Graduate University. His work uses a variety of metholodogy to investigate a range of political economy questions, relating to Intellectual Property Protections, Human Capital Accumulation, China's Political Economy.
He has been published in the Journal of Policy Analysis and Management and the Columbia Journal of History. He has presented at conferences at Duke University, American Historical Association (AHA), and International Congress on Information and Communication Technology (ICICT).
